Overview

Voyage started from a real problem — planning trips with a group of friends means an endless cycle of "when are you free?" messages, calendar screenshots, and someone inevitably missing a date that works for everyone.

The finished app lets users import calendars via .ics (Google, Apple), overlays everyone's busy times on a shared grid, surfaces best availability windows using a custom scoring algorithm, and lets any group member propose date ranges with per-member voting. Once dates are confirmed, the itinerary tab unlocks for day-by-day event planning.

The friend invite system works before the invitee even has an account — invites sent to an email auto-link to a user profile the moment they register. The full REST API covers 34 endpoints across 9 resources, secured with JWT and BCrypt.

Key Features

🗓️
Calendar Availability
Connect Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or import .ics files. The app overlays everyone's busy time and surfaces the best shared free windows automatically.
🗳️
Date Proposals & Voting
Any group member can propose a date range. Everyone votes agree or disagree. When all members agree, the trip dates get confirmed and the itinerary tab unlocks.
📋
Trip Itinerary
Once dates are confirmed, a day-by-day itinerary view opens. Members can add events with time, location, and notes — visible to the whole group or personal only.
👥
Friends & Invites
Invite friends by email — works even if they haven't signed up yet. When they register with the invited email, the friend request appears automatically in their pending tab.
🔒
Auth & Guest Mode
Full JWT authentication with BCrypt-hashed passwords. A guest mode lets visitors browse the app with locked write actions — good for demos and showcasing the UI.
🌙
Dark / Light Mode
Full dark and light theme toggle. The design uses a seafoam teal + sky blue palette with floating geometric shape animations across both modes.
