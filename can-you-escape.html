Can You Escape?

A horror escape room visual novel where players wake up chained in a mysterious room and must solve puzzles across multiple scenes to break free — before time runs out.

Team Team of 2
Duration March 4 – March 14, 2026
Engine Ren'Py + Python
Genre Horror · Puzzle · Escape Room
Overview

The Story

You're walking home alone at night when you take a wrong turn down a dark alley. One bad decision — and you wake up chained to a pipe in a strange room with no memory of how you got there. A masked villain lurks nearby. The clock is ticking.

Can You Escape? is a branching narrative escape room game built in Ren'Py. The game features multiple story paths based on player choices in the intro, then tasks them with solving a series of increasingly difficult puzzles across 5 rooms to find a way out before the 60-minute countdown expires.

Features

What We Built

🔀
Branching Story Choices

Multiple intro paths lead to different story outcomes, giving players agency from the very first scene.

🧩
Multi-Room Puzzles

Combination locks, sudoku, inventory-based clues, puzzle piece collecting, and fax machine puzzles across 5 unique rooms.

⏱️
Live Countdown Timer

A real-time 60-minute timer displayed on screen creates tension and urgency throughout gameplay.

🎒
Inventory System

Players collect items, clues, and puzzle pieces throughout rooms and combine them to progress.

🔊
Ambient Audio

Scene-specific music and sound effects — chains, footsteps, doors — build the horror atmosphere.

🖼️
Custom Artwork

Original AI-assisted artwork for every room, character, and scene creates a cohesive dark visual style.

Tech Stack

Built With

The game was built entirely in Ren'Py — a Python-based visual novel engine. All game logic, puzzle systems, timer mechanics, inventory management, and screen transitions were implemented in Ren'Py's scripting language with custom Python functions.
